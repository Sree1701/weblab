MEA Engineering College, the first NAAC accredited self-financing engineering college in Malappuram district, is committed to provide excellent and value-based education with a flair for ethics and professionalism. The College is located amidst panoramic natural beauty over-looking the hills and valleys at Nellikunnu, near Perinthalmanna – a city of various educational institutions- on the Perinthalmanna- Melattur road. The College is easily accessible by road, rail and air. The College is governed by the MEA sponsored by the “Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama”, a society instituted by top class intellectuals of the Muslim community.The institution is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by the AICTE.
