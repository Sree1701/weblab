About Us

MEA Engineering College, the first NAAC accredited self-financing engineering college in Malappuram district, is committed to provide excellent and value-based education with a flair for ethics and professionalism. The College is located amidst panoramic natural beauty over-looking the hills and valleys at Nellikunnu, near Perinthalmanna – a city of various educational institutions- on the Perinthalmanna- Melattur road. The College is easily accessible by road, rail and air. The College is governed by the MEA sponsored by the “Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama”, a society instituted by top class intellectuals of the Muslim community. Janab Panakkad Sayed Sadique Ali Shihab Thangal is its charismatic head. The institution is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by the AICTE.

MEAEC has been accredited with a "B+" grade by NAAC and holds NBA accreditation for its Computer Science and Engineering and Mechanical Engineering programs. The college promotes a vibrant campus life through various extracurricular activities, such as arts and sports events. It has also engaged in international collaborations, being the only engineering college in Kerala to partner in the MIELES project, which focuses on e-learning strategies.

MEAEC offers undergraduate B.Tech programs in various specializations, including Computer Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. Postgraduate options include M.Tech degrees in Computer Science and Engineering and Power Electronics and Drives, as well as an MCA program. Admission is primarily based on performance in state and national entrance examinations such as KEAM and JEE Main.
